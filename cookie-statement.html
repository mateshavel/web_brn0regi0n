Cookie statement

This site (and all our other sites) uses cookies. A cookie is a small text file stored on your computer containing information on your browser activity. We use use them to evaluate the site’s performance, which helps us improve your experience on the site (we would love to show you more of what you are interested in). A cookie is stored on your computer for up to two years and you can delete all of the many time in your browser settings.

Google Analytics give us information about: 1/the pages you visit on brnoregion.com and how much time you spend here. 2/the sources (where did you come from to our site) and 3/your behaviour (what do you read and what do you click while you are on brnoregion.com). Our websites uses remarketing technology too. This technology enables users who have already visited our page and shown interest in our topics to see targeted advertising about our region.

Facebook Pixel is a tiny piece of Java script code that we have incorporated into each of our web pages. We use custom audience pixels to record information about the way visitors use our website (the user information is always deleted after 180 days, until the user visits our website again). “Custom Audience from Your Website” respects your privacy. Thus, no personal information about individual visitors to the website will be disclosed and we can only advertise to website customer target groups in a targeted way once the customer target group has reached a minimum mass. This makes it impossible for us to discover the identity of individual visitors.

Social platform Twitter also uses cookies other similar technologies, such as pixels or local storage, to help provide you with a better, faster, and safer experience. When you view Twitter content or Twitter products integrated into other websites using Twitter for Websites, Twitter may receive information including the web page you visited, your IP address, browser type, operating system, and cookie information. This information helps us to improve our page, it´s content and services, including personalized suggestions.

Any changes we may make to our Cookie Statement in the future will be posted on this page.
