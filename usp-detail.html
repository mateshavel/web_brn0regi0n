CREATION. WITHOUT DEMOLITION.

Creativity is an indivisible part of life in #brnoregion, not just a hobby of few. Being creative is fun and joy! We love being creative, and support everyone who solves challenges and problems with creativity and enthusiasm. See what the effects and results of such an approach are.

THE SCIENCE!

#brnoregion = 20,000+ employees in science and research, 400+ companies with their own research programmes, 20+ technology development centres at international businesses.

Brno is a growing science metropolis: new science facilities are growing, as is total investment in science and research (among the largest in Europe). More and more scientists keep coming to do top-quality research. The Exhibition Centre hosts the Science, Research & Innovation trade fair every year, and even the youngest children enjoy doing science at the VIDA! Science Centre. Science does wonders in #brnoregion!

tHE GROWTH!

We provide the background for those who make world a better place through intricate innovation.

#brnoregion offers everything you need to grow: the only step you need to take to succeed is to bring your ideas and passions to life. You will get daily support through the precise network of the regional innovation ecosystem and your colleagues and friends. #brnoregion is the perfect background for everyone who believes that gradual innovation is the world’s hope and future.

THE INTERCONNECTIONS!

We connect education, research, and business: it works!

One of the best cards up our sleeves is the knowledge economy. We form connections. The city is just the right size: people know one another so cooperation starts easily and we connect universities with businesses, the private sector with the public sphere, scientific centres with universities. It works. This is proven by the perfectly functioning regional innovation ecosystem as well as the numerous successful local and international companies.
